Lightweight Qt desktop environment.

An unhindered and clear way to go. Focused classic with a modern look and feel.

Try LXQt in most Linux or BSD distributions.
Learn to install it on the LXQt wiki.

Merging the LXDE-Qt and Razor-qt efforts, the project brings good ideas to familiar ones.
Read more about its history on the LXQt wiki.
